Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence Lab - University of Seoul

The Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence Lab (HCAIL) is a research group in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Seoul. Our research interests lie at the intersection of multiple domains: human-centered AI, health informatics, social computing, & accessibility. Our research aimed to support individuals with special needs (e.g., older adults with diabetes, vloggers & streamers with visual impairments, North Korean defectors with depression, Timebank members) through design and engineering across multiple domains: health, social media, sharing economy, and education but not limited to those areas.

We are pursing research in the combinations of the following directions:

  • Artificial Intelligence

  • Health Informatics

  • Human-Computer Interaction
